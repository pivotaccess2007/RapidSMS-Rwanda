To reduce the rate of maternal morality in Rwanda, the Government of Rwanda has recognized that community health workers are necessary in order to improve access to health in rural communities. Each of Rwanda's 15 000 villages has a community health worker devoted to maternal and child health issues.

The Ministry of Health, in cooperation with UNICEF introduces an SMS based system to provide a real time community based surveillance and alert system for maternal and child health.

Rapidsms, a free and open-source framework for dynamic data collection, logistics coordination and communication, leveraging basic short message service (SMS) mobile phone technology was chosen as the technology of choice for the implementation of the project.
